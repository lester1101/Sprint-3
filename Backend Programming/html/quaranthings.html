Spending lockdown moments doing fun and meaningful stuffs

To strike the boredom out, everyone prefers to do hobbies and new kinds of stuff like planting ornamental plants, doing vlogs and tiktoks, having a movie marathon of the latest and the best movies and series through virtual watch together, and everyone turns into being a chef by trying the recipes spreading online.

Chef's kiss to the foods that were guide by online recipes and procedures

Everyone acquired the skills of being a chef by following the recipes which are trending on social media. First on the list, is the iconic Dalgona coffee. Most of the people on social media uploaded pictures of the coffee done by themselves. The second is the mouthwatering Ube Pandesal. I even tried to bake it. It turns out good just like what they are describing in social media. Lastly for this list is the do-it-yourself doughnut. Everyone missed the taste of the glazed donut from the famous doughnut brand, so in order to satisfy the cravings, the recipes on the internet were followed and baked by the online community.
